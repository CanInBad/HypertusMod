
[b]WARNING: This contains hyper stuff, if you dislike it then leave[/b]
This mod adds bodyparts that are infused with "Bluespace anomalies" that enhance them enough to become unstable size wise

Those said bodyparts can be enhanced with perks

This mod has 37 perks in a unique skill tree "Hyper"
said skill tree have 5 tiers unlocks at 0, 5, 15, 30, 50

Some perks might be hidden due to incompatible bodyparts 

There is no story in this version yet, but the perks and bodyparts might not break in the future

Has compatibility layer between these mods:
 - Synth Species
 - Knotted Horse Cock
 - Fluffy Bodyparts
 - Avali Species (both version)
