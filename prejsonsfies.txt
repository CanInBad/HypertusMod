This mods [b]adds[b] [i](not replace)[/i] new body parts that just are like the normal ones but you can upgrade them in a new skill tree that comes with this mod.
Said body parts are in form of breast, penises, vagina, and anus.
This mod also have capability layers that use the looks of your favorite body parts mod, including ones from species mod that add one of the 4 body part categories mentioned.

You can gain experience points of said skill tree by invest in vanilla perks and then sleep, each time you wake up you will be notified how much of them you get.
You can then spend those point to add ridiculous amount of perks to your body parts.
Please do note that some perks might be hidden due to not meeting the requirement for the perk.

There is a draft of the story but it isn't complete in anyway and some glimpse of it can be read in the perks' description.

There are compatibility layers that make all species including the modded one have hyperable version of them

You can transform your or your foe's parts to hyperable with Personal Bluespace Anomaly Emitter™️. You need to finish the Alex's portal quest to get one from his shop
